# MeridianAlgo

**Empowering the world with Open-Source Innovation**

A high-school led nonprofit organization active in 4 countries, creating technology for students, companies, and everyone in between. All our work is 100% open-source, transparent, and community-driven.

## What We Do

- **Financial Literacy Workshops**: Interactive sessions on budgeting, investing, and debt avoidance
- **Ethical Tools**: Open-source technology development
- **Community Partnerships**: Collaboration with schools, youth groups, and NGOs
